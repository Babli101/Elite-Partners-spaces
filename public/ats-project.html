Welcome to L&T Green Reserve
L&T Green Reserve is a premium residential project by Larsen & Toubro Realty, offering a perfect blend of modern living and nature. Thoughtfully designed with eco-friendly features and lush green surroundings, it promotes a lifestyle of comfort, wellness, and sustainability.
Located in a well-connected area, Green Reserve provides residents with a peaceful environment, smart design, and top-class amenities — all while staying close to the city. Live green. Live better — at L&T Green Reserve.
